Main data processing in Music Uncovered

MusicÂ Uncovered purpose is to listen to music and identify the songs. For this purpose the app uses your microphone to access the input audio stream. As an alternative audio source it can also access the audio stream send to your Apple AirPods. In both cases the audiostream is converted into a signature. The signature is a compressed version of the audio stream but it cannot be converted back to the audiostream. The signature can be compared to the known signature of music titles. For that purpose the signature is transferred to the Shazam servers. They try to match your signature with the signature of all the songs in there database. If they find a match, they respond with the song information which includes title, artist, album, release year and the URL of the related coverart. The app then loads the cover art from the URL if available and displays information of the matched song.
