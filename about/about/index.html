About

All about Music Uncovered

Music Uncovered is an app that listens to the music around you and presents cover art and song information in a fullscreen view which will the be updated automatically with every new song. It uses matching colors and a beautiful layout to implement a glancable song display which can be read even from some distance.

As a user of Music Uncovered you can decide if you want to identify the music just once or nonstop. The idea behind the nonstop mode is to have the app running for example in a corner of your table and whenever you are wondering which song is currently playing, just glance at the screen and instantly see all the infos about the song without the need for further interaction. It even works when streaming music to your AirPods.

For each recognized song Music Uncovered presents title, artist, album title, release year and cover art if available.
